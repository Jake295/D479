Coral Bay Resort & Spa

Along the north shore of Yellow Leaf Bay, Coral Bay Resort & Spa offers an island escape. This four-star resort features oceanfront suites, rainforest access, and a spa inspired by native island flowers.

Just minutes from Merriton Landing, the resort is ideal for couples and families looking to relax or explore Taniti.

Choosing Where to Stay

Whether you're a luxury traveler or visiting on a budget, Taniti offers a variety of lodging options to fit your style. Most hotels and resorts are located near Taniti City, providing access to restaurants, entertainment, and the beach.

For nature lovers, campgrounds are available on the outskirts of the island near the rainforest, offering a more secluded and scenic experience.
