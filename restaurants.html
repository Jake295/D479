Coral Flame

Nestled near the Merriton Landing waterfront, Coral Flame offers a fusion of Tanitian flavors and fresh-caught seafood. Its oceanfront terrace, locally sourced ingredients, and vibrant atmosphere make it a must-visit for anyone seeking an unforgettable island meal.
